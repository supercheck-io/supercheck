## Talk to a Person

| Channel | Best for | Availability |
| ------- | -------- | ------------ |
| **In-app chat** | Quick troubleshooting while you are in the dashboard | 24/5 with weekend coverage for critical incidents |
| **Email support@supercheck.io** | Detailed questions, attachments, and follow-up | Response within one business day |
| **Live onboarding** | Guided setup with a product specialist | Tuesdays and Thursdays at 10:00 PT |

## Community & Best Practices

- Join the **Supercheck Community** to share tips, vote on features, and hear about beta programs.
- Attend quarterly **Reliability Roundtables** to learn how other teams scale automated quality.
- Read customer stories on the blog for inspiration and proven playbooks.
