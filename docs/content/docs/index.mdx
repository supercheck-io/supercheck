## Why Teams Choose Supercheck

<Tabs items={['Unified Testing', 'Always-On Monitoring', 'AI Assistance']}>
  <Tab value="Unified Testing">
    Keep browser, API, and custom checks together with reusable variables,
    version history, and shareable results.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Always-On Monitoring">
    Monitor synthetic journeys, APIs, and status pages from one dashboard with
    clear uptime summaries and instant alerts.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="AI Assistance">
    Turn noisy failures into actionable fixes with guided explanations, safe
    change suggestions, and quick re-runs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
