## Why Supercheck

- **All-in-One** — Tests, monitors, alerts, and status pages
- **Self-Hosted** — Deploy on your infrastructure with unlimited features
- **AI-Powered** — Automatic fix suggestions when tests fail
- **Multi-Location** — Monitor from US, EU, and APAC regions
- **Team-Ready** — RBAC, organizations, projects, and audit trails
