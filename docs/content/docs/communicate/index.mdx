Keep customers, stakeholders, and teammates informed with clear, timely updates.

## Overview

Use Supercheck to:

- **Detect & Alert**: Multi-channel notifications with thresholds to avoid noise.
- **Explain Clearly**: Incidents, maintenance, and recovery notes that match your brand.
- **Share Publicly**: Status pages for customers and dashboards for internal teams.

## Common Workflows

### Setting Up Incident Response

<Steps>
  <Step>Connect notification providers (email, Slack, webhooks).</Step>
  <Step>Create alert rules with sensible retry thresholds.</Step>
  <Step>Publish a status page for customers with your logo and domain.</Step>
  <Step>Test the flow by triggering a sample incident.</Step>
</Steps>

### Monitoring System Health

The Dashboard gives a single view of:

- **Runs & Jobs**: Pass/fail, duration, and history.
- **Monitors**: Live status, SSL expiry, and multi-location checks.
- **Capacity**: Queue depth, active workers, and recent errors.
